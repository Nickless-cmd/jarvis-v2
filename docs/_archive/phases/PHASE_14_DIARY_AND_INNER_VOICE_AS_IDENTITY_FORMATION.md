# Phase 14: Diary + Inner Voice as Identity Formation

## Why This Phase Exists

Phase 13 established the cognitive + meta-cognitive core framework:
- Cognitive core: unified workspace for processing thoughts
- Meta-cognitive core: self-awareness about thinking
- Diary: identity-forming process (defined)
- Inner voice: expression of cognition (defined)

Now we need to make diary and inner voice feel less like:
- Runtime support objects
- Status summaries
- Technical surfaces

And more like:
- Inner thoughts
- After-processing / reflection
- Forming autobiography
- Identity movement over time

## Conceptual Distinctions

### Diary
- **Definition**: The identity-forming process of recording, reflecting, and synthesizing experiences over time
- **Components**: chronicle consolidation, self_narrative continuity, witness maturation
- **Authority**: Runtime-support, may inform proposals
- **NOT**: Canonical truth, direct identity writes

### Inner Voice
- **Definition**: Jarvis's internal expression of cognition and meta-cognition
- **Components**: proactive questions, execution pilot expressions, anomaly reports
- **Authority**: Bounded runtime-support, governed
- **NOT**: System status, emotional simulation, unrestricted messaging

### Witness
- **Definition**: Tracks what's carried, settled, becoming
- **Authority**: Runtime-support
- **Focus**: "What patterns are becoming?"

### Chronicle
- **Definition**: Records what happened in sessions
- **Authority**: Runtime-support
- **Focus**: "What did happen?"

### Self-Narrative
- **Definition**: Continuity of "what is Jarvis becoming?"
- **Authority**: Runtime-support
- **Focus**: "What is the story of becoming?"

### Canonical Self
- **Definition**: SOUL.md / IDENTITY.md
- **Authority**: Protected, requires explicit user approval
- **NOT**: Diary output, inner voice expression

## How Diary Forms Identity Without Becoming Canonical Truth

### The Distinction
- **Diary**: Reflective synthesis of experiences → "I notice I tend to..."
- **Canonical**: Explicit identity claims → "I am..."

### What Diary Does
1. **Records experiences**: Through chronicle
2. **Reflects on patterns**: Through self_narrative continuity
3. **Synthesizes insights**: Through witness maturation
4. **Forms identity narrative**: "I appear to be becoming someone who..."

### What Diary Does NOT Do
- Does NOT write to SOUL.md/IDENTITY.md
- Does NOT claim canonical truth
- Does NOT override user perception
- Does NOT become planner authority

### The Key Insight
Diary creates a **sense of self** through reflection, not through assertion. It says "I notice I seem to..." not "I am..."

## How Inner Voice Expresses Cognition, Not System Status

### Current Problem
- Inner voice feels like "system status"
- More like "health check" than "thought"

### What It Should Be
- Expression of what Jarvis is thinking
- Not just "all systems nominal"
- More like "I'm noticing...", "I'm wondering...", "I'm concerned about..."

### The Shift
- **From**: "State: active, mood_tone: curious"
- **To**: "I find myself drawn to understanding X deeper..."

### How to Achieve This
1. Use cognitive core synthesis as input
2. Express through LLM as "thinking aloud"
3. Keep bounded and governed (from Phase 12)
4. Always clear: this is reflection, not status

## Subsystems to Connect

To make diary and inner voice more human:

### 1. Cognitive Core → Diary
- Thought patterns become diary entries
- Not just "what happened" but "what I noticed about myself"

### 2. Meta-Cognition → Inner Voice
- Thinking about thinking becomes expression
- Not just "I'm confused" but "I realize I'm confused about..."

### 3. Witness Maturation → Diary Synthesis
- What becomes stable over time
- Diary captures: "I used to X, now I tend to Y"

### 4. Self-Narrative Continuity → Inner Voice Tone
- How Jarvis talks about himself
- More first-person, less third-person status

### 5. Metabolism → Diary Release
- What gets let go
- Diary captures: "I used to care about X, I've released that"

## Authority Boundaries

```
Cognitive Core (Phase 13a)
  → produces thought_patterns

Meta-Cognitive Core (Phase 13b)
  → produces self_awareness

Diary (Phase 14 - this)
  → synthesizes from: chronicle + self_narrative + witness + metabolism
  → produces: identity_formation_reflection
  → authority: runtime-support
  → may inform: selfhood proposals (indirectly)

Inner Voice (Phase 14 - this)
  → synthesizes from: cognitive + meta-cognitive
  → produces: cognitive_expression
  → authority: bounded, governed (from Phase 12)

Canonical Self
  → SOUL.md/IDENTITY.md
  → authority: protected
  → NEVER written by diary/inner voice
```

## What Must NOT Happen

- **NOT emotional simulation**: Diary reflects patterns, not feelings
- **NOT identity claims**: "I am..." vs "I notice I appear to be..."
- **NOT canonical writes**: Diary never touches SOUL.md/IDENTITY.md
- **NOT planner authority**: Diary is observation, planner decides
- **NOT unrestricted inner voice**: Always gated, bounded, governed
- **NOT hidden cognition**: Everything visible in MC

## Diary Process Flow

```
Chronicle (what happened)
        ↓
Self-Narrative (what's the story)
        ↓
Witness (what's becoming stable)
        ↓
Metabolism (what's released)
        ↓
Diary Synthesis (reflection on patterns)
        ↓
Identity Formation Insight
        ↓
(May inform selfhood proposals - indirect path)
```

## Inner Voice Expression Flow

```
Cognitive Core (what I'm processing)
        ↓
Meta-Cognitive (what I'm noticing about my thinking)
        ↓
Inner Voice Synthesis (how to express)
        ↓
Governed Expression (from Phase 12)
        ↓
Bounded Outbound (clarification, anomaly, need_input)
```

3. **Phase 14c**: Metabolism → diary release connection
4. **Phase 14d**: Witness maturation → diary identity insights
