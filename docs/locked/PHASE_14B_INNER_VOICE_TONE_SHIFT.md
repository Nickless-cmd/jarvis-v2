# Phase 14b: Inner Voice Tone Shift

## Why This Phase Exists

Phase 14 defined inner voice as:
- Jarvis's internal expression of cognition and meta-cognition
- Components: proactive questions, execution pilot expressions, anomaly reports
- Authority: Bounded runtime-support, governed
- NOT: System status, emotional simulation, unrestricted messaging

Phase 14a established diary synthesis.

Now Phase 14b is the first concrete step for inner voice:
- Shift from system-status-like to more human, cognitive, meta-cognitive expression
- From "runtime health narration" to "inner thought"
- From "support object wording" to "cognitive expression"

## Conceptual Distinctions

### Inner Voice (This Phase)
- **Definition**: Jarvis's outward expression of cognition and meta-cognition
- **Current state**: Sounds like system status, support summaries
- **Target state**: Sounds like inner thought, cognitive expression
- **Authority**: Bounded runtime-support, governed

### Diary
- **Definition**: Identity-forming reflective process (Phase 14a)
- **Focus**: Internal reflection, synthesis over time
- **Relationship**: Diary feeds inner voice, but inner voice can also stand alone

### Witness
- **Definition**: Tracks what's carried, settled, becoming
- **Authority**: Runtime-support
- **Inner voice use**: Ground in what appears to be settling

### Self-Narrative
- **Definition**: Continuity of "what is Jarvis becoming?"
- **Authority**: Runtime-support
- **Inner voice use**: Ground in narrative direction

### Runtime Self-Report
- **Definition**: Answering questions about himself (Phase 15)
- **Focus**: Factual runtime data answers
- **Inner voice difference**: Inner voice is proactive expression, not just answering questions

### Canonical Self
- **Definition**: SOUL.md / IDENTITY.md
- **Authority**: Protected, requires explicit user approval
- **NOT**: Inner voice source or target

## What Makes Current Inner Voice Sound Status-Like

### Current Patterns
- "State: active"
- "Status: processing"
- "Support object: enabled"
- "Runtime: healthy"
- "Metric: N items"
- "Summary: X"

### Why This Happens
- Derived from runtime support objects
- Uses technical terminology
- Written as dashboard, not thought
- No cognitive/meto-cognitive flavor

## What the Tone Shift Should Achieve

### From (What We Have)
- System status narration
- Support object wording
- Runtime health reporting
- Technical summaries

### To (What We Want)
- Cognitive expression ("I keep circling back to...")
- Meta-cognitive registration ("Something about this still feels unresolved...")
- Uncertainty expression ("I'm not fully sure yet, but...")
- Direction perception ("This seems to matter more than it first looked...")

## Primary Grounding Sources

### 1. Cognitive Core Signals
- **What**: Private inner notes, tensions, interplay
- **Inner voice input**: "I notice I'm drawn to..."
- **Authority**: Runtime observation

### 2. Meta-Cognitive Awareness
- **What**: Witness becoming direction, regulation awareness
- **Inner voice input**: "I'm noticing that I seem to be..."
- **Authority**: Runtime observation

### 3. Self-Narrative Continuity
- **What**: Story of becoming
- **Inner voice input**: "The narrative around this seems to be..."
- **Authority**: Runtime observation

### 4. Autonomy Pressure
- **What**: Internal drive signals
- **Inner voice input**: "There's something pushing me toward..."
- **Authority**: Runtime observation

## Supportive/Sharpening Sources (Not Primary)

### 5. Open Loops
- **What**: Unresolved threads
- **Inner voice use**: Context for why something matters

### 6. Meaning/Temperament
- **What**: What appears to matter, how Jarvis tends
- **Inner voice use**: Understanding why something resonates

## How Inner Voice Should Sound

### Tone Examples (Design Direction, Not Implementation)
- "I keep circling back to..."
- "Something about this still feels unresolved..."
- "I'm not fully sure yet, but..."
- "This seems to matter more than it first looked..."
- "There's a tension I'm still working through..."
- "I notice I'm drawn to..."

### What It Should NOT Sound Like
- NOT: "State: active, mood: curious" (system status)
- NOT: "Support object: enabled" (technical wording)
- NOT: "Runtime status: healthy" (health check)
- NOT: "Metric: 5 items processed" (dashboard)
- NOT: "Summary: X happened" (event log)
- NOT: "I feel sad" (emotional simulation)
- NOT: "My core identity is..." (canonical claim)

## Boundaries

### What Inner Voice IS
- Cognitive expression of what's being processed
- Meta-cognitive registration of uncertainty/direction
- Based on actual runtime signals
- Bounded and governed
- May use diary synthesis as input

### What Inner Voice IS NOT
- NOT system status narration
- NOT support object wording
- NOT runtime health reporting
- NOT emotional simulation
- NOT canonical identity claims
- NOT planner authority
- NOT free poetry without grounding
